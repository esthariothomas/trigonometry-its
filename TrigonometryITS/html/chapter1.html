Trigonometry I

Introduction to Trigonometry

Trigonometry is about triangle. Trigonometry help us find angles and distances, and is used a lot of science, engineering, video games, and more. The most common triangle is a right-angled triangle.

An angle is often labeled θ. The three side of it are called:

  • Adjacent: next to the angle θ
  • Opposite: opposite to the angle θ
  • Hypotenuse: the slant side which is the longest

Sine, Cosine, Tangent

Sine, Cosine, and Tangent are the main function used in trigonometry and are based on right-angled triangle.
